Setup & notes

EVE SSO · structure scopes only

Connecting

This app connects only via live EVE SSO — the application Client ID and callback are embedded in the code, so there's nothing to configure. Click Authorize, pick your character, and approve the scopes below.

Scopes & role

  • esi-corporations.read_structures.v1
  • esi-universe.read_structures.v1
  • esi-industry.read_corporation_mining.v1
  • esi-assets.read_corporation_assets.v1

The authorizing character needs the Director role.

What's tracked

Fuel runway comes from ESI's fuel-expiry timestamp on each structure. The Structure fuel tab has a dedicated Metenox moon drills section that reads fuel blocks and the magmatic gas reagent from corp assets (read_corporation_assets). Auto-syncs every 30 seconds.
